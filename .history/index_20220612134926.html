COMPANY PROFILE

Lazcom Solar and Electrical Services Limited is a Company registered in Kenya in accordance to chap. 486 of Company’s act. Started in 2009 as a sole proprietorship, Lazcom Solar has grown in customer base and service and it was then registered as a Limited Company in Kenya in 2013. We specialize in design, selection, purchase, servicing, maintenance, replacement, installation, testing and commissioning of all types of green energy solutions up to and including solar water heating system, power back up systems,Borehole drilling and Equiping, solar water pump installation and to carry on business as consultants, advisers and service providers in respect of these and other services.

Vision

To be a leading alternative energy solutions provider by offering affordable and environmental friendly green energy solar solutions beyond the expectations of our clients.

Mission

To satisfy our customers by offering efficient, effective, environmental friendly and affordable electric light, heating and power supplies using the latest technology and state of the art equipment for both commercial and residential use in accordance to our clients' needs.

Advantages of solar water system

  1. Energy Saving
  2. Use of free solar energy hence reduction of power bills.
  3. 24 hours hot water
  4. Long life span-over 20 years

Advantages of power backup systems
  1. Standby power in the event of main’s failure
  2. Alternative to generators which are expensive to maintain and fuel
  3. Instant and automatic changeover when the mains power fails
  4. Totally silent and non-polluting operation
  5. Very low running costs-just the power to charge the batteries
